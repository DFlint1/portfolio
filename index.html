My name is Dana Flint

Hi there! I'm Dana Flint from Austin,Texas. I currently work as a freelance full stack web developer at Tallyso Media. I build mobile apps and websites for both myself and for clients. I have many years experience business development and project management.I have experience programming in different languages such as Javascript, Java, node.js, PHP and JQuery, however some of my favorite projects involve working in Android App Development.

I graduated from The University of Texas at Austin-BA in Psychology and Certificate in Full Stack Web Development. I have an extensive background in business administration including home building and construction. I currently have found my passion in helping people by designing and constructing websites and android apps.
